<p>
I love when things just work! Recently discovered <a href="https://github.com/55sketch/simple-rss">simple-rss</a>, “a  simple JS plugin for adding an RSS feed to your page.” It solved an issue over at <em>bulltown</em> and <em>jenett.neko</em>. 
</p>
<p>
Update 03/09/26: The plugin stopped working at <em>bulltown</em> and <em>jenett.neko</em> - the solution appears to have been only temporary. Oh well. :(
</p>
<p>
For this post, I used the plugin to grab my buddy John’s most recent post, <a href="https://johnjohnston.info/blog/greenside-almost-spring/">Greenside, almost Spring</a> from his feed (I love his photos). I then applied a bit of css and it rendered what you see below. 
</p>
<p>
Important note: For the sake of preserving this post so it doesn’t update automatically the next time John posts, I then copied the rendered result’s html into this post and commented out the code used to grab the feed (see source).
</p>
